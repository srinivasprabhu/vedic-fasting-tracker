# Interaction and haptics

Guidelines for tactile feedback in Aayu.

## Haptics policy

- **Light impact** — Secondary controls: toggles, segment changes, opening pickers, back navigation, quick-add taps (water/steps).
- **Medium impact** — Committed choices: starting or ending a fast, confirming a plan change, saving profile fields.
- **Success notification** — Completed fast (goal met), goal reached (e.g. hydration 100%).
- **Warning notification** — Ending a fast early or other reversible but notable outcomes.

Keep success/warning notifications sparse so they stay meaningful.
